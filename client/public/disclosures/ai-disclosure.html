AI Assistant Disclosure

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Cannavita Budtender uses an experimental AI assistant to provide product guidance and educational information. Responses are generated automatically and may be incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated.

What this assistant can do

  • Suggest products based on your stated preferences.
  • Summarize product categories, effects, and formats.
  • Help you navigate available inventory.

What this assistant cannot do

  • Provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or personalized medical advice.
  • Guarantee efficacy, safety, or outcomes.
  • Replace licensed healthcare professionals or in-store staff guidance.

Recommended use

  • Verify important details with an in-store team member.
  • Read product labels carefully before purchase or use.
  • If you have a medical condition, consult a qualified clinician first.
For urgent health concerns or emergencies, call 911 or contact a licensed medical provider immediately.
